Should I drive or Fly?

This is the question we kept asking ourselves when planning a trip.
However, figuring out the cost and time of each option was a tedious task.
DriveorFly works by making several API calls to find out information about your trip.
In a matter of seconds you can compare the time and cost of flying and driving to your desired destination.

How does it work?

The app will first calculate the driving distance and estimated drive time based on current traffic conditions. It will then obtain the current gas price and calculate the cost of driving.
Next, the app will obtain information on available flights and their associated prices. It will then calculate the estimated travel time.
Once both travel options have been calculated, the app will display a side-by-side comparison of the estimated drive time and cost, as well as the estimated fly time and cost.
This allows you to quickly and easily determine which option is the best for your needs.
